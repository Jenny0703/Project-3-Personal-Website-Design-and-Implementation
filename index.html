ABOUT ME

I am an international student who has come to Canada from China for more than one year. I used to live in Shenyang, China for 18 years. It is a city with a long history and many memories for me. I am a very sensitive person, like traveling, watching the sea, watching movies, taking photos and love animals. But I also like to explore new things, like I made a decision to study abroad, I hope to learn about different perspectives of the world. My dream is to be a director and a team to make many excellent projects and show our ideas to more people.
